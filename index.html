A Note For You

Hi love,
I refuse to believe that the words I love you are like birthday cake. Three words reserved for special occasions only to be given out topped with candles and frostings at the
perfect moment. I use the words I love you casually and recklessly at times, but I found that birthday cake is better at midnight and I love you is better when you don't hesitate when you
don't wait for an occasion to say it. So here it is: "I love you".

And not in the special occasion kind of way. In the coffee in the morning on a Tuesday kind of way. I love you in chalk on a sidewalk and little post-it notes and extra smiles when I
see you. I love you in the language of everyday things. And seeing you at the end of each day and listening to every word you say. I love you in the language of birthday cake...
when it's nobody's birthday.
